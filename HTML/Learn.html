    HTML Syntax of Heading Levels Web Page Outcome
    <h1>This is a H1 Heading.</h1> Main heading of the page
    <h2>Heading 2</h2> Subheading or section heading
    <h3>Heading 3</h3> Subsection heading
    <h4>Heading 4</h4> Sub-subsection heading
    <h5>Heading 5</h5> Minor heading or sub-sub-subsection heading
    <h6>Heading 6</h6> Minor heading or additional information
